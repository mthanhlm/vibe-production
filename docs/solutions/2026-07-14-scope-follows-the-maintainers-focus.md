The suite was removed at its technical best: 201 tests green, two-way
render reviews, a true-engine diagram gate. That is the point of the
learning — polish could not close an intent gap. What survives the
deletion: review the deliverable rather than a proxy (fonts, engines);
hard-gate measured physics, advise on taste; knowledge in references,
choreography nowhere. Those lessons now live in the check/plan skills,
the AI-product standards pack (PD/MEM/TD/EV), and the research
amendments — the loop got permanently better even though the code left.
